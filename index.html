About Me

I'm Prithul Das, an aspiring aerospace engineer currently in my third year of undergraduate studies in Mechanical Engineering at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Bangladesh. My passion lies in merging mechanical engineering principles with aerospace innovation. I focus on aerodynamic analysis, thermal protection systems, and fluid flow optimization. I have experience using Ansys Fluent and am actively exploring COMSOL and OpenFOAM. From studying aerodynamic lift and drag to working on transpirational cooling for reentry vehicles, I'm committed to advancing research in high-speed and hypersonic flight technologies.
