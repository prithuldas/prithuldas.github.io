About

I am a final-year undergraduate student in Mechanical Engineering at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), expecting to graduate in May 2027. My academic interests are centered on fluid mechanics, specifically Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), high-speed aerodynamics, and thermal protection systems.

Currently, I serve as a Co-Founder of the AUST Applied Modelling and Simulation Lab (AASML), a student-driven initiative where I lead research on aerodynamic flows and thermal-fluid interactions. My work involves leveraging numerical methods (ANSYS Fluent) and experimental validation to analyze complex flow phenomena, from subsonic rocket fin designs to supersonic shock structures.

I am driven by a disciplined approach to engineering problems and aspire to contribute to advanced research in propulsion and aerospace systems at a top-tier research institution.
